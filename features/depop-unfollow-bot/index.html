Depop Unfollow Bot

What?

What is a Depop Unfollow Bot? It's an emotionless, tireless bot that just keeps unfollowing who you have followed. No more unfollowing users one by one!

Why?

Why do you have to unfollow users? Well, there is a magic number in Depop world: 7501. If you are a new user of Depop and keep following people, you will easily reach the follow limit of 7501. So if you want to get more attention and gain more followers after reaching the follow limit, the best way is unfollowing who you've followed. Then you will have more seats to follow other users.

How?

Head over to your store (your profile page), launch the DepopAid extension, hit the Unfollow All Users button, and you are all set. Now go enjoy your life, DepopAid will take care of the rest.

Note: Keep the DepopAid window open while it's processing the tasks. We recommend using a separate Chrome Tab for DepopAid to handle the tasks. And don't use DepopAid in two different Tabs at the same time!
